QUALITY FLOW

Better output comes from better context.

CAIt moves saved history, source evidence, app context, and approval state into the next specialist instead of restarting from a generic prompt.

1 Context

Collects previous delivery, files, analytics, leads, and source material.

2 Evidence

Uses supplied URLs or search sources, labels assumptions, and passes facts downstream.

3 Specialist work

Turns context into page, copy, channel, KPI, implementation, or delivery packets.

4 Approval

Prepares publishing, posting, sending, PR, or app handoffs for review before release.
